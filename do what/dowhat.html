Do What?


Place a 9x12 bristle sheet on the flat surface
Place a Prang-16 Semi-Moist Watercolor set right next the bristle sheet.
Grab a paint brush and dip it in water.
With that same paint brush, choose any color that is expressing your emotion with any size brush,
Paint the box as large or as small as you want.

Without cleaning your brush, dab your brush in the color that is on the right side of the last color you choose.
Paint an “X” if you ever fell in love before,
Paint an “O” if not.

Clean your brush.
